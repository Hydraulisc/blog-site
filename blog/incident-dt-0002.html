Incident DT-0002 (Power Grid Outage)

On the 28th of April, 2025, the main Hydraulisc infrastructure experienced an unexpected outage lasting approximately eleven hours. This incident was caused by an unprecedented electrical fault at the grid level, beyond anyone's direct control.


Both UPS systems failed to detect the instability, and didn't issue a shutdown command in time, but luckily, no data loss or corruption appears to have occurred. However, one UPS battery was damaged during the event, and we're currently working through the insurance process to resolve the hardware damages.


Since the last entry (DT-0001), hardware has changed; now running 2*1000W UPS - one for each server.
