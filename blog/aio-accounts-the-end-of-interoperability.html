AIO Accounts: The End of Interoperability

Update (2025-10-21):

Technically OAuth2 allows interoperability.

As per this blog post, Hydraulisc is going to be reborn, with a new, FOSS-focused alternative.

Unfortunately, this means we're having to make some ground-breaking changes.

These changes include, and are not in any way limited to:

  • Replacing All in One (AIO) Interoperable Accounts with OAuth2 scopes
  • The removal of E2EE Private Messaging

Additionally, we're planning a roadmap for feature sets we will add, along with preferred launch and release dates.


This FOSS-Focused alternative aims to bring some clarity to Hydraulisc's inner workings, along with upholding our promise of Free and Open Source alternatives to large social media platforms.
