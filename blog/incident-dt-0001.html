Incident DT-0001 & Hydraulisc Power-Related Outages

Welcome back to another Developers Digest.

Today's article is about "Incident DT-0001", but also goes over general downtime incidents and mitigations.


As many know, Hydraulisc.net's main infrastructure is hosted in SleepingAmi's bedroom. (Server 1, nickname AXIS)


This presents it's own set of challenges, and as such, incidents like DT-0001.


Incident DT-0001

Incident DT-0001 is a downtime incident, which occurred on the 21st of November, 2024. This is the first downtime incident caused by a power outage.

We have fail-safe's in place, such as a 2800W 3000 VA UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply), for short, temporary power outages, but that is also used in the eventuality of a complete power failure to perform a quick save.


Additionally, The Main Hydraulisc Server has redundant backups (4 daily backups, once every ~5.5 hours).
